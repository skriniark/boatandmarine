Maintenance Tips for Keeping Your Boat Looking Great After a Professional Detail


A professional boat detailing service in Wilmington, NC can provide you with the best possible experience when it comes to maintaining the beauty of your boat. From waxing and polishing to cleaning and restoring, these services will ensure that your vessel looks its best! (When you've made the investment in a professional detail, though,) there are also maintenance tips for keeping your boat looking great after the detail has been completed.

First off, it's important not to forget about regular cleaning! Even after a detail, dirt and grime can quickly accumulate on boats if not frequently removed. Make sure to use appropriate cleaners for whatever material is on the surface of your vessel - whether it's fiberglass or wood - and be sure to rinse them off thoroughly afterwards. Additionally, avoid using harsh chemicals as they may damage the surfaces of your boat over time.

Another key tip for maintenance is to apply new wax every couple months. This will help protect against UV radiation from the sun and salt water corrosion from seawater splashing up onto the hull. For wooden vessels, consider applying teak oil every 3-4 weeks during boating season in order to keep wood looking nice and healthy!

Finally, one of the most important aspects of keeping your vessel looking shipshape is proper storage. Wherever you leave your boat when it's not being used should have enough ventilation so that moisture won't collect inside which could cause mold or mildew growth. If you plan on storing outside during winter months, make sure everything is securely covered with a protective tarp or canvas cover! To sum up: With regular care and attention as well as following some basic maintenance tips (such as those above!), you can ensure that your boat stays looking great even after a professional detail!
